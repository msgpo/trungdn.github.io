Discover the power of body language

Body language is a type of nonverbal communication, including facial expressions, gestures, eyes, body postures. According to research, body language accounts for 55% of success in communication, helping the opponent to predict the current state of the person communicating with them. 
Most people don't realize that the majority of real communication is nonverbal communication. Whenever we speak or receive information from outside, our brain immediately sends information to different parts of the body to display the message we are trying to convey or feel. So by observing gestures, postures, facial expressions or even eye movement, you can easily identify exactly what is going on in people's heads. For that reason, body language will be very difficult to fake, but it is likely that we are being misused. To have the right body language you need to understand your own body language first, use it properly to radiate your true energy.
